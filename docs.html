Commands

link / unlink #101

Link your discord account to your Hypixel account. Update your social profile in game if necessary.
Usage: /(un)link (username)
Only your Discord ID and Minecraft UUID are stored.

predict #101

Predict your next prestige level stats and/or your fkdr. It is mandatory to have a session that is already active.
Usage: /predict (fkdr|prestige)
Nothing is stored.

session #101

Create a session for your Bedwars stats. This will allow you to track your stats over time.
Usage: /session
Only your Bedwars stats are stored.

bedwars #101

Get information on your or anyone's Bedwars stats. Each gamemodes are available through the select menu option.
Usage: /bedwars (username)
Nothing is stored.
